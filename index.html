About Our Mess

Our mess system is an independent, student-led initiative. It is not affiliated with the hostel administration or the university authorities. The entire system — from cooking to managing expenses — is handled collectively by the members themselves.

All funding is contributed by the mess members, and all decisions are made jointly. This autonomy helps ensure fairness, transparency, and a sense of community responsibility. Through this self-managed structure, we aim to maintain a balanced, inclusive, and efficient meal service for everyone involved.
